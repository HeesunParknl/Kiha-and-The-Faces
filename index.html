Kiha & The Faces

A unique Korean rock band

Here's their story

The unrivaled star of the Korean indie scene, who have become so famous that the lyrics of one song are included in Korean textbooks. In 2008, their debut song "cheap coffee" quickly emerged as a novel alternative to the Korean music industry, capturing the public with a sense of experimentation and familiarity at the same time. It is a unique band with exciting sounds and folk-rock vocals that are subtly mixed with Korean psychedelic music from the 70s and British New Wave. Jang Ki-ha's unique conversational singing style and realistic lyrics featuring wordplay is very popular. In particular, they are well known for their charisma and passionate stage manners that overwhelms audiences on the live stage.
